✦ Privacy Policy ✦

Whispering Arcana — Last updated April 18, 2026

Whispering Arcana is designed to be a quiet, private space. This policy explains, in plain language, what the app does and does not do with your information.

What we collect

Nothing. Whispering Arcana does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal information. We do not require an account, email, or login of any kind.

How readings work

Every card is drawn randomly in your own browser using your device's built-in random-number generator. Readings are never sent to a server. The app has no backend — there is nowhere for your questions or card draws to go beyond your own screen.

Offline and caching

The app uses a service worker to store its files (HTML, CSS, images, fonts) locally on your device so it works offline and loads quickly. These cached files contain only the app itself — no personal data. You can clear them at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing your browser's site data.

Sound and effects

Ambient sounds and chimes are synthesized in your browser using the Web Audio API. No audio is streamed from a server. You can turn sound on or off at any time using the toggle in the top-right corner.

Fonts

The app loads two typefaces from Google Fonts (Cinzel and Cormorant Garamond). Google may log the IP address used to request the fonts, as it would for any website using Google Fonts. This is covered by Google's Privacy Policy. We do not receive any of that information.

Analytics, advertising, tracking

We do not use analytics, advertising networks, tracking pixels, cookies, or any third-party tags beyond the Google Fonts request mentioned above.

Children

Whispering Arcana is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how the app handles information, we will update this page and change the "Last updated" date above. Because the app is a Progressive Web App, you will automatically receive the updated version the next time you open it online.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent via the app's GitHub repository: github.com/melvoigt/tarot-app/issues.

Entertainment disclaimer

Whispering Arcana is provided for entertainment and reflection only. It is not a substitute for professional advice in medical, legal, financial, psychological, or other domains.
